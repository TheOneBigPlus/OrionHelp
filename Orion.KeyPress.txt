Send a key press to the client (triggering of the client's macros and the assistant's hotkeys without window focus).<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>bool Orion.KeyPress</b>('key');<br>
<b>bool Orion.KeyPress</b>('key', ctrl);<br>
<b>bool Orion.KeyPress</b>('key', ctrl, alt);<br>
<b>bool Orion.KeyPress</b>('key', ctrl, alt, shift);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>key</b> - a string or an integer. A set of characters to press (for example Orion.KeyPress('asd') - will <i>alternately</i> press a, s, d)<br>
        or the number of the button to press (for non-printable buttons, such as F1. Example: Orion.KeyPress(112) will press F1)<br>
    <li><b>ctrl</b> - boolean - hold Ctrl<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>false</b><br>
    <li><b>alt</b> - boolean - hold Alt<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>false</b><br>
    <li><b>shift</b> - boolean - hold Shift<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>false</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
