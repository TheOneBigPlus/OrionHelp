Check for the possibility of a step.<br>
<hr><br>
<b>bool Orion.CanWalk</b>('direction', x, y, z);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b><a href="search:Directions">direction</a></b> - string - direction for step<br>
    <li><b>x</b> - integer - X coordinate in the world where we take a step<br>
    <li><b>y</b> - integer - Y coordinate in the world where we take a step<br>
    <li><b>z</b> - integer - Z coordinate in the world where we take a step<br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> boolean - true if you can take a step there.
