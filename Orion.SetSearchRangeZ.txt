Set limits for searching by Z coordinate.<br>
<hr><br>
<b>Orion.SetSearchRangeZ</b>(minZ, maxZ);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
<li><b>minZ</b> - integer - minimum Z coordinate<br>
<li><b>maxZ</b> - integer - maximum Z coordinate<br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
