Get the ping value calculated by the client (only when the ping check option is enabled).<br>
<hr><br>
<b>int Orion.GetPing</b>('name');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>name</b> - string - ping mode<br>
        Options:<br>
        <b>'min'</b> - minimum ping<br>
        <b>'max'</b> - maximum ping<br>
        <b>'average'</b> - average ping value<br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> integer - ping value (9999 if the ping was not requested/received).
