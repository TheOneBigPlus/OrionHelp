Get all fake semi-transparent static objects added to the world map.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>Orion.FakeMapObjectsList</b>();<br>
<b>Orion.FakeMapObjectsList</b>(map);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>map</b> - integer - the ID of the map/facet<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>current map</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> string list - serial numbers of added fake objects.
