Buff/debuff description.<br>
<hr><br>
<b>String Orion.BuffDescription</b>('name');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>name</b> - string - names or graphics of the buff/debuff picture to check<br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> string - buff description.<br>
If no buff will return<b>""</b>.<br>
