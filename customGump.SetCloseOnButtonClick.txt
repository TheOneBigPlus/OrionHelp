Make the custom gump automatically close when the button is selected.<br>
By default, custom gumps are not closed when buttons are selected (as server-side does).<br>
<hr><br>
<b>customGump.SetCloseOnButtonClick</b>(state);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>state</b> - boolean - state true/false<br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
