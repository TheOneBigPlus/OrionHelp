Attempt to take a step in a specified direction.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>bool Orion.Step</b>('direction');<br>
<b>bool Orion.Step</b>('direction', run);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b><a href="search:Directions">direction</a></b> - string<br>
    <li><b>run</b> - boolean - true for running<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>false</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> boolean - true if an attempt is made.
