Turn off the computer (or try to do it).<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>Orion.ShutdownWindows</b>();<br>
<b>Orion.ShutdownWindows</b>('mode');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>mode</b> - string - shutdown mode<br>
        Can be <b>'forced'</b><br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>''</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
