Wait for a prompt from the server.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>bool Orion.WaitForPrompt</b>();<br>
<b>bool Orion.WaitForPrompt</b>(delay);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>delay</b> - integer - max wait time, in milliseconds<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>1000</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> boolean - true if prompt is received from the server.
