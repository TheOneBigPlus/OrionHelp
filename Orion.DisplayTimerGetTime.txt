Get the remaining lifetime of the display timer.<br>
<hr><br>
<b>int Orion.DisplayTimerGetTime</b>('id');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>id</b> - string - display timer ID<br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> integer - the remainder of the timer display time (in milliseconds). If -1 is returned, then there is no timer in the client.
