Get your character's bitflags.<br>
<hr><br>
<b>int Player.Flags</b>();<br>
<hr><br>
List of flags:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>0x01</b> - frozen
    <li><b>0x02</b> - unknown
    <li><b>0x04</b> - poisoned or flying (for clients 7.0.0.0+)
    <li><b>0x08</b> - yellow hits
    <li><b>0x10</b> - ignore characters
    <li><b>0x20</b> - locked
    <li><b>0x40</b> - war mode
    <li><b>0x80</b> - hidden
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> integer - player bitflags.
