Set the light level.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>Orion.SetLight</b>(state);<br>
<b>Orion.SetLight</b>(state, value);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>state</b> - boolean - true to turn on the light filter<br>
    <li><b>value</b> - integer - light level from 0 to 31<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>current lighting level from the server</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
