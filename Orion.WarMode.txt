Switch war mode state<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>Orion.WarMode</b>();<br>
<b>Orion.WarMode</b>(state);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>state</b> - boolean<br>
        0 - switch off<br>
        1 - switch on<br>
        2 - flip the current state<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>2</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
