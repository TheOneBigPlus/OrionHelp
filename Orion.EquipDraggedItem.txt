Equip the item from the character’s hand.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>Orion.EquipDraggedItem</b>();<br>
<b>Orion.EquipDraggedItem</b>('container');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>container</b> - string - container serial number<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>'self'</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
