Wait for a context menu to open<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>bool Orion.WaitForContextMenu</b>();<br>
<b>bool Orion.WaitForContextMenu</b>(delay);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>delay</b> - integer - maximum time to wait, in milliseconds<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>1000</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> boolean - true if the context menu is received.
