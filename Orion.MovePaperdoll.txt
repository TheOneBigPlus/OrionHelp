Move character's paperdoll gump.<br>
<hr><br>
<b>Orion.MovePaperdoll</b>('serial', x, y);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>serial</b> - string - the serial number of the character<br>
    <li><b>x</b> - integer - X coordinate on screen where to move paperdoll<br>
    <li><b>y</b> - integer - Y coordinate on screen where to move paperdoll<br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
