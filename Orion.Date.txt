Get current date.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>String Orion.Date</b>();<br>
<b>String Orion.Date</b>('format');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>format</b> - string - date format<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>'dd.MM.yyyy'</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> string - current date in the specified format.
