Get the current season.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>int Orion.Season</b>();<br>
<b>int Orion.Season</b>('type');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>type</b> - string - the type of the received value can be: 'real' (sent by the server), 'current' (used by the assistant)<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>'current'</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> integer - the value of the specified season type.
