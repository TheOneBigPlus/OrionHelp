Remove character from list for custom highlighting.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>Orion.RemoveHighlightCharacter</b>('serial');<br>
<b>Orion.RemoveHighlightCharacter</b>('serial', priorityHighlight);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>serial</b> - string - character serial number<br>
    <li><b>priorityHighlight</b> - boolean<br>
        <b>true</b> - high priority of illumination and will always highlight the character with this color, regardless of the attacked/poisoned state and others.<br>
        <b>false</b> - low priority of highlighting (if the character is attacked/poisoned or has other conditions - it will be painted by them).<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>false</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
