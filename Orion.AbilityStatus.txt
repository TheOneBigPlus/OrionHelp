Get ability status.<br>
<hr><br>
<b>bool Orion.AbilityStatus</b>('name');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>name</b> - string - name or serial number of the ability<br>
        Can be <b>'Primary'</b> or <b>'Secondary'</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> boolean - true if the ability is activated.
