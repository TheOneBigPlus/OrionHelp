Waiting for the delivery of packets to the server and receiving a response (by clicking on the backpack).<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>Orion.CheckLag</b>();<br>
<b>Orion.CheckLag</b>(delay);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>delay</b> - integer - maximum delay (milliseconds)<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>1000</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
