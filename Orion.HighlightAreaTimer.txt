Get the remaining lifetime of the highlighted area.<br>
<hr><br>
<b>int Orion.HighlightAreaTimer</b>('id');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>id</b> - string - area id;<br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> integer - the remainder of the area display time (in milliseconds). If 0 is returned, then there is no area in the client.
