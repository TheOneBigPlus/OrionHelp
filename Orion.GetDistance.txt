Calculate distance to an object OR a coordinate<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>int Orion.GetDistance</b>('serial');<br>
<b>int Orion.GetDistance</b>(x, y);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>serial</b> - string - serial number of the object<br>
    <li><b>x</b> - integer - X coordinate in the world<br>
    <li><b>y</b> - integer - Y coordinate in the world<br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> integer - tile distance to the object/coord.
