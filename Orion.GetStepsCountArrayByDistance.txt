Checking the possibility of passage to the specified range of coordinates by distance relative to the position of the character in the world.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>StepsCounterObjectList Orion.GetStepsCountArrayByDistance</b>(maxDistance);<br>
<b>StepsCounterObjectList Orion.GetStepsCountArrayByDistance</b>(minDistance, maxDistance);<br>
<b>StepsCounterObjectList Orion.GetStepsCountArrayByDistance</b>(minDistance, maxDistance, openDoor);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>maxDistance</b> - integer - maximum distance from the character<br>
    <li><b>minDistance</b> - integer - minimum distance from the character<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>0</b><br>
    <li><b>openDoor</b> - integer - door opening mode<br>
        <b>0</b> - do not open<br>
        <b>1</b> - always open<br>
        <b>2</b> - only depending on client Auto Open Doors option<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>2</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> array of objects <b>StepsCounterObject</b>, for each tile for which a route was attempted.
