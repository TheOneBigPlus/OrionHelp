Wait for a trade window to open/refresh<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>bool Orion.WaitForTrade</b>();<br>
<b>bool Orion.WaitForTrade</b>(delay);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>delay</b> - integer - maximum wait time, in milliseconds<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>1000</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> boolean - true if the trade window opened
