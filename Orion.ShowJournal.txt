Display journal contents in a text window.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>Orion.ShowJournal</b>();<br>
<b>Orion.ShowJournal</b>(linesCount);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>linesCount</b> - integer - number of lines to output.<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>maximum number of journal lines</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
