Get the name of the other character making the trade.<br>
<hr><br>
<b>String Orion.TradeName</b>('serialOrIndex');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>serialOrIndex</b> - string - trade window index. Starts from 0, or serial number<br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> string - character name.
