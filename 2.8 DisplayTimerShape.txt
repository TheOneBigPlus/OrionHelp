<h1>Timer Shapes</h1>
<hr>
Specify the shape of your displayed timers.<br>
<br>
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>Circle</b>
    <li><b>Rectangle</b>
    <li><b>Line</b>
    <li><b>Bar</b> - The timer will be animated and change as time passes.
    <li><b>Vertical</b> - Change the orientation.  Only for <b>Rectangle</b> or <b>Line</b> timers.
</ul>
<hr>
Shapes can be combined.<br>
For example: 'Rectangle|Bar|Vertical' or 'Circle|Bar'.
