Get a list of enemies in one line.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>String Orion.EnemyListToString</b>();<br>
<b>String Orion.EnemyListToString</b>('separator');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>separator</b> - string - the delimiter used to split the serial numbers<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>'|'</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> string - a list of enemies, condensed to one line, and split by the specified separator.
