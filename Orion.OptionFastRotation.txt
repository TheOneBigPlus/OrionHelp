Get or change the Fast Rotation option.<br>
<hr><br>
Getting value:<br>
<b>bool Orion.OptionFastRotation</b>();<br>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> boolean - true if the option is enabled.<br>
<hr><br>
Changing value:<br>
<b>Orion.OptionFastRotation</b>(state);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>state</b> - boolean - <i>true</i> to enable the option<br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
