Equip an item by type from the players backpack and pouches (with the recursive search option enabled).<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>Orion.EquipT</b>('graphic');<br>
<b>Orion.EquipT</b>('graphic', 'color');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>graphic</b> - string - graphic of item to search<br>
    <li><b>color</b> - string - color of item to search<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>'any'</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
