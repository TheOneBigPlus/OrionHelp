Get enemy list.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>StringList Orion.GetEnemyList</b>();<br>
<b>StringList Orion.GetEnemyList</b>(names);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>names</b> - boolean - true to get names, false to get serial numbers<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>false</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> string list - list of enemy names/serials
