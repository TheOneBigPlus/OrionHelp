Cast spell.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>Orion.Cast</b>('spellNameOrIndex');<br>
<b>Orion.Cast</b>('spellNameOrIndex', 'targetSerial');<br>
<b>Orion.Cast</b>('spellNameOrIndex', 'targetSerial', relativeTargetDistance);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>spellNameOrIndex</b> - string - spell name or number<br>
    <li><b>targetSerial</b> - string - spell target serial number<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>call the target to specify the target manually</b><br>
    <li><b>relativeTargetDistance</b> - integer - distance for pointing the target at the tile relative to the specified character, depending on its direction<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>0</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
