Get the light level.<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>int Orion.Light</b>();<br>
<b>int Orion.Light</b>('type');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>type</b> - string - Can be: 'real' (sent by the server), 'current' (used by the assistant), 'personal'<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>'current'</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> integer - the value of the specified lighting type.
