Start sailing on a boat (new boat control system).<br>
<hr><br>
Call variants:<br><br>
<b>bool Orion.SailOnBoat</b>('direction');<br>
<b>bool Orion.SailOnBoat</b>('direction', fast);<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b><a href="search:Directions">direction</a></b> - string - direction to move<br>
    <li><b>fast</b> - boolean - true to move at top speed<br>
        <i>Default value</i>: <b>false</b><br>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
