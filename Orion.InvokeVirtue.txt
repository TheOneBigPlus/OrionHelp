Invoke one of the 8 virtues<br>
<hr><br>
<b>Orion.InvokeVirtue</b>('name');<br>
<hr><br>
Arguments:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>name</b> - string<br>
        Possible values: <i>compassion, honesty, honor, humility, justice, sacrifice, spirituality, valor</i>
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> does not return a value.
