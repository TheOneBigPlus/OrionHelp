Get object bitflags.<br>
<hr><br>
<b>int obj.Flags</b>();<br>
<hr><br>
Flags:
<ul style="margin-left: -20px">
    <li><b>0x01</b> (1) - frozen
    <li><b>0x02</b> (1) - unknown
    <li><b>0x04</b> (1) - poisoned or flying (for clients 7.0.0.0+)
    <li><b>0x08</b> (1) - yellow hits
    <li><b>0x10</b> (1) - ignore characters
    <li><b>0x20</b> (1) - locked
    <li><b>0x40</b> (1) - war mode
    <li><b>0x80</b> (1) - hidden
</ul>
<hr><br>
<b>Result:</b> integer - object bitflags.
